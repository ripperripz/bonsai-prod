# 🖼️ Quick Guide: Update Serenity Gallery Images

## Simple 3-Step Process

### Step 1: Login to cPanel
- Go to your hosting cPanel
- Click **File Manager**

### Step 2: Navigate to Images Folder
- Open: `public_html/images/serenity/`

### Step 3: Upload New Images
- Click **Upload** button
- Select your images
- **Must use these exact names**:
  - `2.png` - Incense burner
  - `3.png` - Moss texture  
  - `4.png` - Billiard table
  - `5.png` - Water feature
  - `6.png` - Stone landscaping
  - `7.png` - Bonsai tree
  - `8.png` - Olive tree trunk
  - `9.png` - Wood grain
  - `10.png` - Minimalist interior
- Choose **Overwrite** when prompted

## ✅ Image Requirements
- **Size**: 800x800 pixels (square)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **File Size**: Under 500KB
- **Names**: MUST match exactly as shown above

## 🔄 After Upload
- Clear browser cache: `Ctrl+F5` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
- Refresh website to see new images

## 📁 Other Image Locations

**Homepage Gallery**: `public_html/images/` → Files: `1.png` to `15.png`
**Designer Photo**: `public_html/images/designer.png`
**Logos**: `public_html/images/bonsai*.png`

---

**That's it!** No coding required. Just upload and overwrite. ✨
